About

Welcome to Phoenix Academy Tuition Classes, where education meets excellence! Established in 2017, we have been committed to providing top-notch academic support and guidance to students from grades 5 to 12 and beyond. At Phoenix Academy Tuition Classes, we believe in nurturing a student-centric learning environment that fosters individual growth and academic success.

At Phoenix Academy Tuition Classes, we understand that each student is unique, with distinct learning needs and pace. Therefore, our classes are designed to be student-centric, offering individualized attention and support. Our small class sizes enable teachers to focus on each student's strengths and areas for improvement, ensuring holistic development. Our commitment goes beyond academics; we are dedicated to supporting the overall growth and well-being of every child.

Classes we offer

  • Classes from 5 to 12
  • 11th and 12th - Accountancy, Economics, and Business Studies
  • B.Com, BBM, MBA
  • CA, CS, and ICAI (Entrance)
  • Crash course for board exams
  • Special classes for supplementary exams
